About IEEE SB GECI

IEEE SB GECI is a newly formed student branch that has come to effect on Dec1,2019. By introducing a student branch, we are opening a world of possibilities, accessible to each and everyone who aspires to learn and evolve. Our main motive is to evolve students in a way that help the entire community to develop alongside. The team spirit and cooperation that was shown towards us till date ,is responsible for the milestones we have achieved so far.
